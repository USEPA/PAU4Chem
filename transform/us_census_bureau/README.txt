Statistics_of_US_businesses_2004.csv is the 2004 Statistics of U.S. Businesses (SUSB) from the U.S Census Bureau. This was used because
the sample frame for the 2005 PACE survey was extracted from the 2004 Business Register
Link: https://www.census.gov/data/datasets/2004/econ/susb/2004-susb.html
Date: 12 June 2020

Pollution_Abatement_Capital_Expenditures_2005.csv and Pollution_Abatement_Operating_Costs_2005.csv
were obtained from the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's Pollution Abatement Costs and Expenditures - 2005 Survey
Link: https://www.epa.gov/environmental-economics/pollution-abatement-costs-and-expenditures-2005-survey
Date: 12 June 2020

ASM_2008.xlsx is the 2008 Annual Survey of Manufactures (ASM). It is used to gather total cost of
shipments for manufacuring sector
Link: https://www.census.gov/data/tables/2008/econ/asm/2008-asm.html
Date: 15 June 2020

SUSB_2008.csv is the 2008 SUSB. It is used to gather information about total establishment for manufacturing sector
Link: https://www.census.gov/data/datasets/2008/econ/susb/2008-susb.html
Date: 15 June 2020

Sampled_establishments.csv was obtained after running Population.py
